Privacy Policy for SandDrop

Welcome to SandDrop, a game developed by Trenchverse.

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our game.

1. Information We Collect

We collect limited information to provide and improve the game experience:

a. User Information

  • Wallet address (used for transactions and gameplay rewards).

b. Gameplay Data

  • Scores.
  • Leaderboard data.
  • In-game actions.

c. Transaction Data

  • Blockchain transaction details (handled via MiniPay or wallet provider).
  • We do not store private keys or sensitive wallet credentials.

2. How We Use Information

We use collected data to:

  • Display leaderboard rankings.
  • Enable gameplay features.
  • Process in-game rewards and purchases.
  • Improve game performance and user experience.

3. Third-Party Services

SandDrop uses third-party services:

  • MiniPay (for payments and wallet interaction).
  • Blockchain networks (for transactions).

These services may collect data according to their own privacy policies.

4. Data Storage

  • We do not store sensitive financial information.
  • Gameplay and leaderboard data may be stored on our servers or third-party services.
  • Blockchain transactions are publicly visible by design.

5. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your data, but:

  • No system is 100% secure.
  • Blockchain transactions are irreversible.

6. Children's Privacy

SandDrop is not intended for users under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

7. Your Rights

You may:

  • Stop using the game at any time.
  • Request deletion of your data (if applicable).

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy. Updates will be posted on this page.

For any questions, contact:

Email: contact@trenchverse.com
