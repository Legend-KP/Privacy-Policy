Privacy Policy for Trenchverse Games

Welcome to Trenchverse. This Privacy Policy applies to all games and apps published by Trenchverse.

It explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our games.

1. Information We Collect

We collect limited information to provide and improve the game experience:

a. User Information

  • Account details you provide (such as username or email, if applicable).
  • Wallet address or payment identifier (if you connect a wallet or use in-app payments).
  • Device and usage information needed to operate our games.

b. Gameplay Data

  • Scores.
  • Leaderboard data.
  • In-game actions.

c. Transaction Data

  • Payment and transaction details processed through third-party wallet or payment providers.
  • We do not store private keys, passwords, or other sensitive payment credentials.

2. How We Use Information

We use collected data to:

  • Display leaderboard rankings.
  • Enable gameplay features.
  • Process in-game rewards and purchases.
  • Improve game performance and user experience.

3. Third-Party Services

Trenchverse games may use third-party services such as:

  • Payment processors and wallet providers.
  • Hosting, analytics, and infrastructure providers.
  • Blockchain or digital payment networks (where applicable).

These services may collect and process data according to their own privacy policies. We encourage you to review those policies when you use linked payment or wallet features.

4. Data Storage

  • We do not store sensitive financial information.
  • Gameplay and leaderboard data may be stored on our servers or third-party services.
  • Some transaction records (for example, on public blockchains) may be visible by design.

5. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your data, but:

  • No system is 100% secure.
  • Some payment or blockchain transactions may be irreversible.

6. Children's Privacy

Our games are not intended for users under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

7. Your Rights

You may:
